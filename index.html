About

I'm an M.Sc. student in Computer Science at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, working in the Vision, Inference, and Learning (VIL) Lab, under Dr. Oren Freifeld's supervision. Expected graduation: Summer 2022.  My research focuses on unsupervised methods for video analysis with applications such as Multiple Object Tracking, Intrinsic Image Decomposition, Video Superpixel Segmentation, and Moving-camera Background Modeling. I'm interested in Deep learning, Bayesian nonparametric models, unsupervised learning, and optimization. 

I'm looking for my next research challenge and eager to bring my mathematical and computational knowledge to create state-of-the-art solutions.
